Artificial Ecology Simulation

Welcome to this AE that simulates a predator-prey relationship.

1. Seaweed (Prey species)

  • Grows 150 points each update, cannot move
  • Reproduces when energy reaches 550 points

2. Fish (Predator species)

  • Loses 70 points each update, can move
  • Gains energy all points of the seaweed it eats
  • Reproduces when energy reaches 1000 points
